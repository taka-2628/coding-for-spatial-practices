Pretzel in Hortus Deliciarum

Pretzel has its origin around 610 A.D. in the region between the south of France and northern Italy. It then became popular and spread into Austria and Germany.

During the Middle Ages, monks gave away pretzels to the poor as a religious symbol that additionally provided literal sustenance.
