King Akbar and Chapati

There are many stories behind the history of Chapati.

Some say that Chapati came from the Egyptian Indus Valley civilisation 5000 years ago. Others claim it was founded in East Africa and brought over to India.

The most common evidence is that it was founded in Southern India. Chapati is mentioned in old Sanskrit text from over 6000 years ago. It is also said it was a favourite of King Akbar way back in 1556.
