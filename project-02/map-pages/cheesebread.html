Cheese Bread

Cheesebread is a small, baked cheese roll or cheese bun, a popular snack and breakfast food in Brazil

Minas Gerais — an interior state bordering Rio de Janeiro — is credited with refining the recipe. Some have acknowledged late Brazilian president Itamar Franco, who held the country’s top political office in the early ‘90s, as the figure who helped popularize his home state’s go-to snack on a national level.
